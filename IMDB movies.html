Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

The data is gotten from Udacity, it is about Hollywood movies in different genres from 1960 to 2015

It has 10866 rows and 21 columns

Columns¶

  • id
  • imdb_id
  • popularity
  • budget
  • revenue
  • original_title
  • cast
  • homepage

Questions for Analysis¶

  • What kind of movies had highest budget and revenue?
  • Is duration affect the popularity of movies?
  • Who is the most famous director?
  • What is the most famous production company?
  • How many movies are Documentary?
